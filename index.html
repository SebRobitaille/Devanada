OUR APPROACH

We approach every project with a 4 step process, ensuring that we cover all aspects of your website development.

1

Define

2

Design

3

Develop

4

Deliver

1

Define


After we’ve identified the brand colors, fonts, and feel of the website, we will use Figma to quickly create a variety of mockup designs to determine which layouts and formats would work best for your growing business. Using Figma, we’ll be able to identify the different UI elements needed such as buttons, menus, forms and more on an interface design.

2

Design


A cohesive user interface is a key component to a successful website. By creating brand consistency across the site, adding animations and features to the UI, we can make a site that is not only visually appealing, but also easy for users to navigate.

3

Develop


Devanada's front-end developers master modern, cutting-edge technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript to create beautiful websites that adapt easily to different platforms and devices. Our developers take your vision and turn it into reality one line of code at a time.

4

Deliver


Our extensive planning phase ensures that the end product aligns with the goal of capturing customers. In the product development phase, we conduct rigorous usability tests that ensure the website is perfect prior to launch. We follow up with a thorough quality assurance process, which includes cross browser testing, responsive design testing, and performance optimization. We also thoroughly test for SEO compatibility and responsiveness across devices to ensure important features are easily accessible across all platforms.
